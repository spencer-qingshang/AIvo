感谢信

**亲爱的露西，**
Dear Lucy,
**我写这封信是为了感谢你的善意，因为在我准备英语期末考试时，你给了我很大的帮助。**
I am writing this letter to thank you for your kindness, because you help me a lot when I was preparing for my final English examination.
**事实上，你的笔记和学习方法真的非常有帮助。一方面，你记的笔记不仅清晰，而且非常实用。另一方面，你的学习方法让我的复习变得十分高效。**
In fact, your notes and study methods were more than helpful. On the hand, the notes were not only clear but also useful. On the other hand, your study methods made my revision efficient.
**再次感谢你的帮助。希望你能接受我的谢意。期待收到你的回信。**
Thank you again for your help. It's my hope that you can accept my gratitude. I look farward to hearing from you.
**你的朋友，** **王林** ***
