**I'm a professional woman.**

我是一个职业女性，有一个五口之家——我的丈夫和三个孩子——需要养育，所以我每周都要购买大量的杂货。相比之下，我发现线上杂货超市和实体杂货店都有各自的优点和缺点。

**The major online grocery supermarket is set up to provide the most benefit to those who buy a lot of items and return frequently.**

主要的线上杂货超市指在为那些经常购买大量商品

**It offers convenience, time and energy saving, and at reasonable prices.**

它提供了方便，节省了时间和精力，而且价格合理。

**But its delivery charges are necessary unless my order is large enough to qualify for free delivery.**

但是，除非我的订单足够大以达到免运费的标准，否则仍需支付配送费用。

**But if I were a housewife who doesn't value time saving, uses lots of coupons, and goes to two or three different physical grocery stores to pick up bargains, I would prefer the physical store in the neighborhood.**

但如果我是一个不太看重节省时间的家庭主妇，使用大量优惠券，前往两三家不同的实体杂货店寻找优惠，我会更偏好邻近的实体店。

**Where — aisle and shelf — to look for just about everything I would be likely to buy — to buy with my heart at rest.**

在那里，我可以放心地在各个过道和货架上寻找我可能想要购买的几乎所有东西。

**But it takes time to get there and return home, especially to get through the long checkout line.**

但到那里并返回家中需要时间，尤其是在长长的结账队伍中等待。

**Besides, I would spend more money than expected each time because of the attractive goods on hand.**

此外，每次我都会因为眼前诱人的商品而花费比预期更多的钱。

**On the whole, I prefer to shop on the Net grocery supermarket.**

总的来说，我更喜欢在网络杂货超市购物。

**As I'm always busy with my profession, time is as precious as gold.**

由于我一直忙于工作，时间如金子般宝贵。

**As I'm always tired, I enjoy sitting before the computer and having my groceries delivered at the door, only with a mouse clicked in any hand.**

由于我总是感到疲惫，我享受坐在电脑前，只需点击鼠标，就能将杂货送到家门口的感觉。

**That's the feeling I really enjoy very much.**

这种体验让我非常享受。
