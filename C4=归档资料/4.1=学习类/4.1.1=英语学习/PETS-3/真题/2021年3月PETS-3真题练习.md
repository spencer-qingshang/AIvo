## SECTION I Listening
**(25 minutes)**

### Part A
1. What is the new benefit of green tea?
[A] Reducing cancers.
[B] Preventing strokes.
[C] Improving memory.
[D] Protecting the heart.
**【正确答案】：C**
**原文：** M: The article said scientists have found a new benefit of green tea. W: I've been told that green tea can lower the risks of heart disease, stroke, and cancers. M: Besides that, it's good for memory, and it helps slow down aging of the brain.

2. What does the man suggest the woman do?
[A] Finish her report.
[B] Have a short rest.
[C] Stand up to work.
[D] Adjust the screen.
**【正确答案】：B**
**原文：** W: Oh, my neck is stiff. M: You should stand up and stretch your legs. Who can sit in front of the screen for 10 hours without a break? W: Come on, Jim. You know my report is due tomorrow.

3. What is the possible relationship between the two speakers?
[A] Husband and wife.
[B] Doctor and patient.
[C] Boss and secretary.
[D] Teacher and parent.
**【正确答案】：D**
**原文：** W: I've been worried that Richard is frozen. M: What seems to be the problem? W: Well, he has trouble concentrating when getting along with other children. I was wondering there might be something on his mind. M: Some problem at home?

4. Why does the man eat out so often?
[A] He has no time to cook.
[B] He likes restaurant food.
[C] He has to treat his friends.
[D] He needs to do that for business.
**【正确答案】：D**
**原文：** M: Sally, why are people now do business more and more? W: Right. I have to be on the flight every week. M: Wow, I really envy you. W: Don't envy me. In fact, I'm sick of restaurant food. Sometimes I just want a home-cooked meal.

5. What does the man think of the music?
[A] It is boring.
[B] It is relaxing.
[C] It is stimulating.
[D] It is discouraging.
**【正确答案】：B**
**原文：** W: Oh, just look at these people. They are really listening to the music. They're driving me mad. M: Can't you see that this is the real communication between the musician and the audience? Listen to our music. It belongs to our generation. W: Well, it may be your music, but it certainly isn't mine. I'm going for a cup of coffee.

6. What does the woman mean?
[A] She did not hear what was shocking.
[B] She is not sure how to solve the mystery.
[C] She was not able to manage the project well.
[D] She feels surprised how she could finish so early.
**【正确答案】：D**
**原文：** M: I was shocked when I heard you had finished your research project a whole month earlier. W: How I managed to do it? It's still a mystery to me.

7. What does the woman's problem mainly concern?
[A] Language ability.
[B] Financial support.
[C] Social contacts.
[D] Cultural differences.
**【正确答案】：A**
**原文：** M: How long have you been in this country? W: Two months exactly. M: Is it hard for you to study at the American College? W: Yes, I find it very difficult, especially when I cannot follow the professor's lectures. M: That's because you just arrived.

8. What does the woman think that the man did wrong?
[A] He spent all day emailing.
[B] He left his email page open.
[C] He forgot to close the door.
[D] He quarreled with his boss.
**【正确答案】：B**
**原文：** W: Why are you so upset, David? M: My boss warned me not to spend all day communicating with my friends, but I didn't. He just didn't believe me. W: You must have forgotten to close your Hotmail window again. You should have known better.

9. What does the woman think of the man's new suit?
[A] It is expensive.
[B] It is fashionable.
[C] It is a good bargain.
[D] It is the best choice.
**【正确答案】：C**
**原文：** M: What do you think of my new suit? W: Not bad. It reminds me of the one I saw at the new department store last week. Did you get it there? M: No, I got it in that big shopping center. It cost me only $150. W: Well, I don't think it's a good bargain.

10. Why did the woman's mother get annoyed with her father?
[A] He fell asleep while watching TV.
[B] He complained about her cooking.
[C] He changed channels from time to time.
[D] He stuck to his own favorite TV program.
**【正确答案】：C**
**原文：** W: My mother got really annoyed with my father yesterday evening. M: Why? Did she complain about his cooking? W: Oh no. It was about watching television. M: Don't tell me he fell asleep in front of the television. W: No, he kept changing channels on the remote.
