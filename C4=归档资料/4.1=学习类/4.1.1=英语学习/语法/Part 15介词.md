# 表示地点的介词

- 前面和后面

- in front of

- The bus stop is in front of the book store.

- behind

- The door is behind the sofa.

- 介词要放在名词前

- 上面和下面

- on,over

- on，紧贴着表面

- An apple is on the desk.

- over，有一定距离的上面

- A picture is over the desk.

- beneath,under

- beneath，紧贴着表面

- The ring should be somewhere beneath the snow.

- under，有一定距离的下面

- A dog is under the desk.

- 在......

- in，用于国家、城市等广阔的地方

- I was born in China.

- at，用于在相对于狭窄的地方

- I am at my friend's house.

- 在......旁边

- by，紧挨着

- She is by the tree.

- next to，按顺序的下一个

- My house is next to her house.

- beside，挨着，并排

- A tree is beside the house.

- 上面三个语义上并没有什么严格区别。

- 在......之间

- between，两者之间用

- The bakery is between the bank and the post office.

- among，三者及以上用

- She is among a lot of people.

- in,at,on

- in可以简单地理解为三维立体空间

- in 2002,in 1992

- at指具体的点

- I am at the bank.

- on是表示接触的介词

- put it on the desk.

# 表示方向的介词

- 朝......,向......

- to，（向，朝着）

- Come to my house.

- I go to school.

- from...to...（从......到......，表示起点和终点）

- from A to Z.

- from cradle to grave.

- Draw a line from point A to point B.

- for (朝着......,表示目的地)&to ward (朝......方向)

- They ran to ward the west.

- 终点不明确

- This is the train for Boston.

- along (沿着......)

- Go along the main street.

- across(横穿)&through(贯通)

- We walked across the ice.

- 向里，向外

- into，进入......中

- I went into the room.

- out of，从......中出去

- I went out of the room.

# 表示时间的介词

- int&at&on

- In

- In 2002

- In May

- In summer

- at

- at 8 o'clock.

- at noon.

- on

- on Sunday.

- on Christmas

- on May 1st.

- in&within

- in，在......后，在......左右

- He will come back in 10 days.

- 10天后回来

- within

- He will come back within 10 days.

- 10天内回来

- before&after

- before，在......之前

- I washed my hand before dinner.

- after，在......之后

- I play basketball after school.

- until&by

- until,表示动作的持续或状态的持续

- She waited for you until five o'clock.

- You must not go until I come back.

- He ran on and on until he was completely tired out.

- by，但有时间终止的意思，完成的意思

- Can you repair my computer by Thursday?

- I will be here by five o'clock.

- I usually finish work by six o'clock.

- for&during

- for+表示具体时间长度的名词

- for an hour / two hour / half an hour.

- We played soccer for four hours.

- We watched TV for three hours last night.

- for a day / ten days.

- for a week / three weeks.

- for a mounth / four months.

- for years.

- I lived in L.A. for five years.

- for centuries.

- during,表示“在某段期间内”的意思，后面常接表示时间段的名词

- during + 表示时间段的名词

- during the movie

- I fell asleep during the movie.

- during the vacation

- We're going to vietnam during our vacation.

- during the night

- during the summer

- during the trip

# 其他介词

- 表示拥有的介词,of (......的)

- the history of China.

- The door of the car is broken.

- 表示目的、原因的介词for （为了......，因为......）

- a house for rent.

- I will do my best for you.

- 表示原因的介词of,from (因为......)

- He died of a heart attack.

- She died from the injuries.

- 表示工具、方法的介词 by，with

- He cut the apple with a knife.

- I go to school by bus.

- with表示使用（工具），by表示使用（手段）

- with (和......一起)和without (没有......)

- I had a race with him.

- He went out without a hat.

- about (关于，大约)

- We talked about the project.

- He came about four o'clock.

- 复合介词，两个以上单词构成的介词

- in front of

- because of

- Because of your help,I could succeed.

- in spite of

- In spite of the difficulties of the moments,I still have a dream.

- 介词后面要跟名词，如果要跟动词则用动名词，如果用动词不定式就会变成两个介词连一起了

- Thank you for visiting China.
