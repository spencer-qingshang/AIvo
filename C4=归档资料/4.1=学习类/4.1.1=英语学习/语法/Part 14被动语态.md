句子大致分为两种一种自己主动去做某事，另一种是受别人指示去做某事。

# 被动语态的结构

英语中用be+p.p表示被动语态

She is loved by me.

被动语态的句子中可以同时存在be动词和实义动词，只不过实义动词需要变形。

动作不是由主语完成的，而是由其他人完成的，就用被动语态

第一类句型和第二类句型没有宾语无法用被动语态。

# 第三类句型的被动语态

- 结构

- 第三类句型：主语+谓语+宾语=我做什么

- 被动语态：什么（宾语）被我（主语）做

- 关键

- 主动语态中的宾语变成被动语态中的主语

- 主动语态中的谓语变成被动语态的be+p.p.

- 主动语态中的主语在被动语态中变成by+宾语

- 现在时的被动语态

- 例句

- Sarth teaches English.

- 将宾语放到句首

- English

- 谓语变成be+p.p.

- English is taught...

- 主语变成by+宾语

- English is taught by Sarah.

- He washes his car.

- His car is washed by him.

- Bees collect honey.

- Honey is collected by bees.

- 过去时的被动语态

- They painted those pictures.

- Those pictures were painted by them.

- Columbus discovered the America.

- The Americas was discovered by Columbus.

- She wrote the book.

- The book was written by her.

- 将来时的被动语态

- She will finish the work.

- The work will be finished by her.

- Jane will clean the room.

- The room will be cleaned by Jane.

- He will write a letter.

- A letter will be written by him.

- 进行时的被动语态

- 谓语变be+being+p.p.

- She was washing the car.

- The car was being washed by her.

- They are building the house.

- The house is being built by them.

- The members of the committee are considering our plan.

- Our plan is being considered by the members of the committee.

- People are cutting down the trees.

- The trees are being cut down by people.

- 完成时的被动语态

- Jane has cleaned the room.

- 谓语变have/has been+p.p

- The room has been cleaned by Jane.

- The hurricane has destroyed dozens of houses.

- Dozens of houses have been destroyed by the hurricane.

- Somebody has stolen my key.

- My key has been stolen by somebody.

- 否定句的被动语态

- She did not write the card.

- 在be动词后加not

- The card was not written by her.

- The old' don't like pop songs.

- Pop songs are not liked by the old.

- They don't like their English teacher.

- Their English teacher is not liked by them.

- 含助动词的被动语态

- She can not play the piano.

- 谓语变助动词+be+p.p.

- The piano can not be played by her.

- He can't read the book.

- The book can't be read by him.

- He may miss the train.

- The train may be missed by him.

- 含疑问句的被动语态

- 一般疑问句

- Did you find book?

- 想象成陈述句

- （Did) you find book?

- 变被动语态

- The book was found by you.

- 变疑问句

- Was the book found by you?

- Does she clean this room?

- Is this room cleaned by her?

- 特殊疑问句

- When did you build this house?

- 想象成陈述句

- (When did) you build this house?

- 变成被动语态

- This house was built by you.

- 变成疑问句

- Was this house built by you?

- 变成特殊疑问句

- When was this house built by you?

- by you可以省略

- When was this house built?

- Where did she find this book?

- Where was this book found?

# 第四类句型的被动语态

- 两个宾语都可以作主语

- She teaches us English.

- 间接宾语（人）作主语

- We are taught English by her.

- 直接宾语

- English is taught us by her.

- 为了表达更明确“给某人”，可以在“某人”前加上to

- English is taught to us by her.

- You gave Shrek the book

- Shrek was given the book (by you).

- The book was given Shrenk (by you).

- The book was given to Shrek(by you).

- Shrek gave Princess Fiona the ring.

- Princess Fiona was given the ring by Shrek.

- The ring was given to Princess Fiona by Shrek.

- My girfriend gave me a present.

- I was given a present by my girfriend.

- A present was given to me by my girlfriend.

- 注意事项

- She made me a cake.

- A cake was made me by her.

- 间宾前只能用for不能用to

- A cake was made for me by her.

- 人作主语的被动语态

- made,write,send,bring,read,buy,sell等这些词只能事物作主语

- I was made a cake by her.

- 我被他做成了蛋糕

# 第五类句型的被动语态

- 第五类句型的被动语态

- They call me a genius.

- 宾语提前

- I

- 谓语变be+p.p.

- I am called

- 宾补放在谓语后

- I am called a genius...

- 主语变"by+宾语"

- I am called a genius by them...

- I think him a doctor.

- He is thought a doctor by me.

- We called him a spy.

- He was called a spy by us.

- We elected Bush a President.

- Bush was elected a President by us.

- You make me crazy.

- I am made crazy by you.

- made意思是“做,使成为”，所以可以用人做主语。

- 含感官动词、使役动词的被动语态

- 特点：动词可以作补语

- 例子

- I was made work hard by her.

- 错误，made不是使役动词无法跟动词work

- 生活中为了简便会省略to，语法上称为省略to的动词不定式

- He makes me wash his car.

- My boss had me finish the job.

- 如何知道是省略了to呢？需要变成被动语态

- She made me work hard.

- I was made to work hard by her.

- We saw him break the window.

- He was seen to break the window by us.

- have,resemble,become

- 表达拥有的不能变成被动语态

- 还有类似

- disappear

- appear

- escape

- happen

- reamin

- exit

- take place

- consist of
