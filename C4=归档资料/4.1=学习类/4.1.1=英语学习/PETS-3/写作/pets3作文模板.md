I am writing this letter to thank you for your kindness, because you helped me a lot when I was preparing the final English examitaion.
**事实上，你的笔记和学习方法真的非常有帮助。一方面，你记的笔记不仅清晰，而且非常实用。另一方面，你的学习方法让我的复习变得十分高效。**
In fact, your notes and study methods were more than helpful. On one hand, the notes you took were not only clean but also useful. On other hand, your study methods made my revision efficient.

Thank you again for your help. It's my hope that you can accept my gratitude. I look forward to helping from you.

**你的朋友，** **王林** ***
Yours,
Wang lin
