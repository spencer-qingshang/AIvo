# 比较级

- 比较级结构

- 通常在形容词或副词结尾+er+than

- English is easier than Chinese.

- A cat is bigger than a mouse.

- She is more beautiful than you.

- Mike is a better swimmer than I.

- He has more books than you.

- She drinks more milk than her baby.

- than后面原本也可以加包含主语和谓语的从句

- I can speak English more fluently than my brother speaks English.

- 英语中than后面的内容和前面的句式相同会省略than后面的

- 比较级中的that&those

- The population of China is larget than the population of USA.

- 可以用that或者those替换the population

- The population of china is larget than that of USA.

- The climate of Korea is mider than taht of Canada.

- The birth rate in 2010 was lower than that of in 1960.

- 比较级的一致

- “比较的对象必须一致”

- The nights are longer in winter than summer.

- 错误，在英语中冬天和夏天不相同，所以不能比较

- The nights are longer in winter than in summer.

- 比较级的强调

- 强调比较级时要用much，still，far，even，a lot + 比较级

- I fell much / still / far / even / a lot better.

- Ted runs a lot faster than his brother.

- That's still better.

- She speaks Japanese far better than I / me.

- This flower is even prettier than that.

- very不能用于强调比较级

- 在比较级前加the的三种情况

- 用于表示“两个中更......的”时

- This is the heavier of these two bags.

- 比较对象很多时

- 某个群体

- the greater part of...

- the latter part of...

- the younger generation

- the upper class

- 其中一个

- His family belonged to the upper class.

- The latter part of his play was so boring.

- 习惯用语中的“the+比较级”

- The more,the better.

- The sooner,the better.

- The higher we climb,the colder it becomes.

- The more we talked,the closer we seemed to grow.

- The more we know about him,the better we can understand his novels.

# 最高级

- 最高级的结构

- 三者以上比较时用最高级，“the + 最高级”或“the most...”

- Tom is the oldest of them.

- Bill is the smartest in his class.

- Winter is the coldest of the four seasons.

- Seoul is the biggest city in Korea.

- Cheetahs run the fastest of all the animals.

- in，表示“在”的意思，一般接表示地点或团体的名词；

- the + 最高级 + in + 地点、团体 （the world,this class,my family）表示"在......中"

- She is the most beautiful girl in my class.

- of，表示“......的”的意思，所以后面要接表示人（动物）成员的名词。

- the + 最高级 + of + (人、动物)成员（them,all the students,all the animals）

- She is the most beautiful girl of all the students.

- 有两种比较级和最高级形态的双音节单词

- quiet

- quieter,more quiet

- quietest,most quiet

- clever

- cleverer,more clever

- cleverest,most clever

- simple

- simpler,more simple

- simplest,more simple

- 最高级中不加the的情况

- 范围不确定时不加the，要用不定冠词

- Jane is the most beautiful girl in our class.

- Jane is a most beautiful girl.

- 所有格前不加the

- He is her the youngest child.

- Jane is my most beautiful friend.

- 在同一对象中比较不加the

- This lake is the deepest in the world.

- This lake is deepest at the point.

- 副词的最高级前不加the

- I get up eaarliest

- Those who mount highest can see farthest.

- 从比较级变为最高级

- 用最高级

- Jack is the tallest boy in the class.

- Seoul is the biggest city in Korea.

- 用比较级表达最高级

- 比较级+than any other +单数名词来表达最高级的含义，意思是“比其他任何......更......”

- Jack is taller than any other boy in the class.

- Seoul is bigger than any other city in the Korea.

- 比较级+than all the other + 复数名词来表达最高级，意思是“比其他所有......更......”

- Jack is taller than all the orther boys in the class.

- Seoul is bigger than all the other cities in Korea.

- 还可以用“否定词+比较级”表达最高级的含义，意思是“没有任何人（事物）比......更......”

- No (other) boy in the class is taller than Jack.

- No (other) city in Korea is bigger than Seoul.

- Nobody in the class is taller than Jack.

- Nothing in Korea is bigger than Seoul.

- 用原级表达最高级“否定词（no one/nothing等） + as...as”

- No(other) boy in the class is as tall as Jack.

- No(other) city in Korea is as big as Seoul.

# 原级

- 原级比较的结构

- as...as...，和.......一样

- She is as tall as he.

- My mother is as old as my father.

- She is as pretty as her sister.

- 在原级前加上not就成比较级

- This is not as long as that.

- Jack is not as smart as Nick.

- the same & the same as

- the same

- 表达完全一样

- The suitcases are all the same size.

- Love is being tuned into the same wavelength.

- the same as...

- 表达和......一样

- The rest is the same as above.

- My conclusion is the same as yours / your conclusion.

- The profits in 2010 were the same as those in 2009.

- the same as + 从句

- You look the same as you did 10 years ago.

- I think the same as you do about this.

- I bought the same computer as you did / bought.

- This is the same watch as I lost.

- This is the same watch that I lost.

- 比较数值时使用...times + as + 形容词 + as...

- ......的几倍

- This box is four times as big as that one.

- European unemployment rates are twoor three times as high as those in the US.

- This river is two times as long as that river.

- =This river is twice as long as that river.
