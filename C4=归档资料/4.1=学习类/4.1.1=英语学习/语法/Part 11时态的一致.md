# 时态的一致

- 含名词性从句的复合句

- 主句要用现在时，从句可以是任意一种时态

- 主句式现在说话者对听者说的时间点

- 从句是想表达的事件发生的时间点

- 主句的谓语：一般现在时

- I know that Shrek loves Princess Fiona.

- 我知道史莱克爱菲奥娜公主

- 从句是一般现在时

- I know that Shrek loved Pricess Fiona.

- 我知道史莱克爱过菲奥娜公主

- 从句是一般过去时

- I know that Shrek will love Princess Fiona.

- 我知道史莱克会爱上菲奥娜公主

- 从句是一般将来时

- I know that Shrek has gone to Las Vegas.

- 我知道史莱克已经去了拉斯维加斯

- 从句是现在完成时

- 如果是过去完成时需要有特定过去的时间点的副词，会很复杂，几乎不用。

- 主句的谓语：现在完成时

- I have know that shrek loves Princess Fiona.

- 我已经知道了史莱克爱菲奥娜公主

- 从句是一般现在式

- I have known that Shrek loved Princess Fiona.

- 我已经知道史莱克爱过菲奥娜公主

- 从句是一般过去时

- I have known that Shrek will love Princess Fiona.

- 我已经知道了史莱克会爱上菲奥娜公主

- 从句是一般将来时

- I have known that Shrek has loved Princess Fiona.

- 我已经知道了史莱克爱上了菲奥娜公主

- 从句是现在完成时

- 主句的谓语：一般将来时

- You will know that Shrek loves Princess Fiona.

- 你会知道史莱克爱菲奥娜公主

- 从句是一般现在时

- You will know that Shrek loved Princess Fiona.

- 你会知道史莱克爱过菲奥娜公主

- You will know that Shrek will love Princess Fiona.

- 你会知道史莱克会爱上菲奥娜公主

- 从句是一般将来时

- You will know that Shrek has loved Princess Fiona.

- 你会自动史莱克已经爱上了菲奥娜公主

- 从句是现在完成时

- 主句的谓语：一般过去时

- 过去时只能用过去式和过去完成时

- 特例的情况下，从句要用现在时

- 不变的真理

- We learned that the earth moves round the sun.

- I knew that the sun rises in the east.

- 现在的习惯

- He said that he goes to church every Sunday.

- Tony told me that Shrek gets up early every day.

- 人尽皆知的普遍事实

- She told me that two and three makes five.

- I learned that ligh travels faster than sound.

- 如果用现在时或未来式，那样句子会很奇怪，你过去不可能知道现在的事情或者未来的事情

- I knew that Shrek had loves Princess Fiona.

- 我知道了史莱克爱过菲奥娜公主

- 从句是一般过去时

- I knew that Shrek loved Princess Fiona.

- 我知道了史莱克爱上了菲奥娜公主

- 从句是过去完成时

- I though that we could win the game.

- I knew that Shrek was sick.

- I knew that Shrek had been sick.

- 历史事件时，无论主句是什么时态，从句都用过去时

- My teacher says that World War II broke out in 1939.

- I know that World War II came to an and in 1945

- I know that Columbus discovered America.

- 含定语从句的复合句

- 主句的谓语：一般现在时

- This is the man who helps me now.

- This is the man who helped me yesterday.

- This is the man who will help me tomorrow.

- This is the man who has helped me since 2000.

- 主句的谓语：一般将来时

- I will help the man who lives in Seoul Station now.

- I will help the man who lived in Seoul Station then.

- I will help the man who will live in Seoul Station from tomorrow.

- I will help the man who has lived in Seoul Station since 2000.

- 主句的谓语：一般过去时

- I helped the man who lived in Seoul Station then.

- I helped the man who had lived in Seoul Station then.

- 不变的真理

- I know the fact that the earth goes around the sun.

- I knew the man who goes to church every Sunday.

- 历史事件

- I know the fact that Columbus discovered America.

- 含状语从句的复合句

- 主句的谓语：一般现在时

- I study English hard because I major in English.

- I study English hard because I majored in English two years aog.

- I study English hard becaues I have majored in English for two years.

- I study English hard because I will major in English next year.

- 主句的谓语：一般将来时

- I will study English hard because I major in English.

- I will study English hard because I majored in English two years ago.

- I will study English hard because I have majored in English for two years.

- I will study English hard because I will major in English next year.

- 主句的谓语：现在完成时

- I have studied English hard because I major in English.

- I have studied English hard because I majored in English two years ago.

- I have studied English hard because I have majored in English for two years.

- I have studied English hard because I will major in English next year.

- 主句的谓语：一般过去时

- I studied English hard because I majored in English two years ago.

- I studied English hard because I had majored in English for two years.

- 状语从句和主句的时态一般是一致的

- 易混淆

- ”条件和时间状语从句用现在时代替将来时“只适用主句是将来时态，其他时候保持主谓一致

- “条件和时间状语从句用现在时代替将来时”只适用于状语从句

- 条件和时间的状语从句与名词性从句、定语从句的区别

- I don't know the truth.

- 用when he will come代替宾语

- I don't know when he will come.

- 用when he will come here again

- I don't know the day when he will come here again.

- 用状语从句时，从句可有可无

- I will let you know his arrival when he comes here again.

- 因为从句可有可无，虽然表达将来但是英语为了避免重复选择用现在时态
