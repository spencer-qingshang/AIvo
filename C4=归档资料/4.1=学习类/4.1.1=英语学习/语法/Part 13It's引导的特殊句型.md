# It's time句型

- It is time + 动词不定式

- It is time to go

- It is time + 从句

- 谓语动词用动词的过去式，但是表达的意思是现在时

- It is time that you went to bed.

- 用于表达责备或不满

- 与虚拟语气对比

- 都是表达没有发生的事情

- 都要动词过去式，但是翻译成现在时

- 区别

- 不遵循同类原则

- 动词只用过去式

- 实义动词

- 虚拟语气过去时

- If you bought it,you might regret it.

- If she had enough money,she could buy a new car.

- If he heard the news,he should be glad.

- It is time...句型

- It's time (that) you went to bed.It's late.

- It's time (that) you got up.It's already 10 o' clock.

- It's high time (that) we cleaned the window.It's very dirty.

- be动词

- 虚拟语气过去时

- If I were you,I would not belive what he says.

- If you were rich,I could buy the car.

- If he were at home,I would tell him about it.

- It is time...句型

- It's high time that this room was properly cleaned!

- It's time the children were in bed.It's long after their bedtime.

- 用助动词should

- It's time that you should go to bed.

- should可以省略

- 即使是第三人称也要用go，而不是用goes，注意。

# 忠主要命提+should句型

- 忠告

- I advise/recommend that you immediately should go on a diet.

- 表示忠告，从句必须用should，但是可以省略

- 主张

- I insisted that Shrek should meet Pricess Fiona.

- 表达主张，必须做单现在还没做的事情必须用should，但是可以省略

- 要求

- His teacher asked that Shrek should study hard.

- 命令

- I ordered that Shrek should take this medicine three times a day.

- 提议

- I suggest that you should eat dinner.

- S+V+that+S+V

- 主句有表示忠告等的动词，that从句内容不是描述过去，而是必须做的意思，必须用should+动词原词

# It's+判断/感情形容词+that+should

- 表示判断的形容词

- 用表示必要和需要的形容词

- natural

- important

- necessary

- 例子

- It is natural that Tom should marry Jane.

- It is time (that) Tom should marry Jane.

- 以上表达，还没有实现的事情，表示不久的将来

- "It is + 判断形容词+that"

- It is necessary that Shrek (should) exercise regularly.

- It is natural that he (should) be indignant.

- It is important every child (should) be happy.

- It is important that you (should) study hard.

- 表示感情的形容词

- 感情的形容词

- wonderful

- surprising

- strange

- sad

- 表达不久的将来将要实现

- 例子

- It is strange that he (should) say so.

- It is strange that you (should) trust such a lie.

- It is strange that she (should) trust such a lie.
