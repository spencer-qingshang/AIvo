# PETS-3 写作模板 (10篇)

#重点 #PETS3

> [!quote] 考试套路
> PETS-3 写作分为 A、B 两部分。A 节为通知、书信等应用文（100词左右），B 节为议论文（120-150词左右）。掌握以下万能模板可确保基础分。

---

## 一、应用文模板 (A节)

### 1. 建议信 (Letter of Advice)
**[开头句]** I am writing this letter to offer some suggestions concerning...
**[核心段]** In my opinion, it would be wise to... Furthermore, you may find it helpful to...
**[结尾句]** I hope you will find these proposals useful.

---

## 二、议论文模板 (B节)

### 2. 对点阐述型 (Pros and Cons)
**[开头]** Recently, the problem of ... has been brought into focus. Opinions vary from person to person.
**[观点 A]** Those who are in favor of this idea believe that...
**[观点 B]** On the contrary, others hold the view that...
**[我的总结]** As far as I am concerned, I am inclined to the former/latter...
