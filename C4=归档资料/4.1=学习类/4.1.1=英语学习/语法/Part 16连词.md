**什么是连词？**

起连接作用的词。

I like apples and bananas.

如果没有连词，句子会很啰嗦，有了连词会让句子简练。

I like apples,I like bananas.

连词可以把相同词性的词练在一起

- 名词

- apples and bananas

- 形容词

- beautiful and wonderful

- 副词

- easily and quickly

- 动词

- relax and sleep

- 短语和句子

- a red delicious apple and long yummy bananas

- I like apples and you like bananas.

# 句子的种类

- 简单句

- 只有一个主语和一个谓语的句子

- I am a student.

- I like a banana.

- 并列句

- 与and，or，but等两个简单句构成的句子

- He is tall and he is smart.

- Speech is silver but silence is gold.

- I will go to Europe this summer or I will take summer classes.

- 复合句

- 主句+从句构成的句子

- 名词性从句

- 名词在句子作主宾补，含主语和谓语的从词在句子中作主宾补

- That you are a doctor is the important thing.

- I know that you are a doctor.

- The important thing is that you are a doctor.

- 定语从句

- 形容词修饰名词，含主语和谓语的从句在句子中修饰名词时

- This is the man who helped me yesterday.

- I know the man who is a doctor.

- 状语从句

- 副词修饰形副动句，含主语和谓语和状语从句在句子中修饰形副动句时

- I am sorry that you are not here.

- He ran faster than I expected.

- Call me if you need my help.

- As you know,she is very pretty.

- If it rains,I will cancel the plan.

- I will tell the truth when she comes back.

- 并列复合句

- 含有简单句、并列句、复合句的句子。

- His cloth was dirty,and his food was scraps which other people had thrown away.

# 并列连词

- 并列连词的种类

- and

- “和“

- I like pizza and hamburgers.

- He and she go to the theater.

- I like studying English and French.

- I like pizza,hamburgers,apples and bananas.

- but

- ''但是“，用于连接与前面相反的内容

- I like pizza,but she likes hamburgers.

- I studied,but she went to the party.

- yet

- "仍然"，用于前后内容转折的意思

- I'm tired,yet I can't sleep.

- He is poor,yet he is satisfied with his life.

- or

- "或者，否则"的意思，表示二者选一的连词

- Do you like an apple or a banana?

- I want to drink water or juice.

- Study hard,or you will regret it.

- so

- "所以，因此"的意思，用于说明前面的事情引起的结果，只能连句子

- It was so cold,so I didn't go out.

- I was sick,so I went home early.

- nor

- and not的意思，用于连接否定的内容

- Not a man,nor a child is to be seen.

- He nor I plan to attend any social function this year.

- He can't see,nor can he hear.

- =He can't see,and he can't hear either.

- for

- "因为......"的意思，用于说明理由，只能连接句子

- I don't like physics,for it is difficult.

- I visit the grocery store every day,for there are lots of fresh things.

- 区分易混淆的连词和介词

- for和because的区别

- I don't like physics,for it is difficult.

- I don't like physics,because it is difficult.

- 一个是并列连词，一个是从属连词

- 并列连词前一定要逗号，从属连词则有无逗号都可以

- because和because of的区别

- because of后面通常接短语，because后通常接句子

- I went to bed early because I was sick.

- I don't go out because it's raining.

- I was late because of a traffic jam.

- although (=though)和despite

- "尽管"

- despite是介词

- I love her despite her faults.

- although是引导从句的连词

- Although he is not famous,he is a creative author.

- Although I'm broke,I don't need your help.

- despite和in spite of的区别

- 后面一般接名词，构成介词短语

- Despite( = In spite of) our efforts,we failed to win the prize.

- He attended the meeting despite (= in spite of) his illness.

# 从属连词

- 引导名词性从句的从属连词

- that,whether/if和特殊疑问词都可用作从属连词

- That

- It is true that Shrek married Princess Fiona.

- I know that Shrek married Princess Fiona.

- The important thing is that Shrek married Princess Fiona.

- whether/if

- Whether Shrek married Princess Fiona is important.

- I wonder whether Shrek married Princess Fiona.

- The important thing is whether Shrek married Princess Fiona.

- 间接疑问句中的特殊疑问句

- Who she is is not important.

- I don't know who she is.

- The important thing is who she is.

- 引导状语从句的从属连词

- 时间状语从句

- When I read a book,I am happy.

- 原因状语从句

- Because I was tired,I went to bed early.

- 让步状语从句

- Though I was tired,I studied hard last night.

- 条件状语从句

- If you turn to the left,you will see the building.

# 关联连词

- both

- 两者都

- He is both a novelist and a poet.

- I can both cook and sew.

- 作主语时用are

- Both she and I are not wrong.

- either

- 两者之一的情况

- I don't want either tea or coffee.

- I'II try either off or on.

- Everyone's mobile was either busy or turned off.

- I want you to answer with either yes or no.

- You must either email or write to him.

- 作主语时选离最近的主语一致

- Either you or he is wrong.

- Either he or I am to blame.

- neither

- 用于否定两者的情况

- He is neither a scholar nor a politician.

- She neither ate nor drank for days.

- The water was neither hot nor cold.

- 作主语时用最近原则

- Neither she nor I don't have any plan for the weekend.

- Neither I nor they are busy.

- Neither man nor mouse does not need air.

- not only

- 不仅，而且

- He is not only poor but also lazy.

- She can read not only English but also Japanese.

- She was not only intelligent but also very musical.

- It is not only beautiful but also useful.

- 特例

- also可以省略 （= not only A but B)

- Not only my sister but I am to go abroad.

- not merely或not simply代替not only

- not only放在句首时，句子要倒装

- Not only was he a noted author but also a gifted speaker.

- Not only has man managed to land on the moon,but his standard of living has changed to one of relative wealth and luxury.

- ......as.......as......

- 不仅B，而且A

- She eats rice as well as noodles.

- She is bright as well as beautiful.

- I know your faults as well as your virtues.

- I could learn a lot from people as well as from books.

- He has experience as well as knowledge.

- He is a poet as well as a scholar.

- 作主语时，采取最远主语原则，强调A而作的附加说明B

- He as well as you is responsible for it.
